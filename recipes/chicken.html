Chicken

Information

Prep Time: 10 mins

Fry Time: 1 hrs 5 mins

Rest Time: 10 mins

Total Time: 1 hrs 25 mins

Servings: 8

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons poultry seasoning
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 (5 pound) whole chicken, giblets removed

Directions

  1. Preheat an air fryer to 360 degrees F (180 degrees C) according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  2. Whisk olive oil, Italian seasoning, poultry seasoning, paprika, and salt together in a small bowl.
  3. Pat chicken dry using paper towels. Brush spice mixture over entire chicken, including underneath the wings.
  4. Place chicken in the basket of the air fryer break side down; cook, undisturbed, for 45 mins. Carefully flip the chicken over using tongs; cook until an instant-read thermometer reads 165 degrees F (74 degrees C), about 20 mins. Remove chicken to a cutting board; let rest for 10 minutes.
