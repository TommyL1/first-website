Egg salad

Information

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 15 mins

Additional Time: 10 mins

Total Time: 35 mins

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 8 Eggs
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard
  • Green onions, chopped
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Paprika

Directions

  1. Boil, peel, and chop the eggs.
  2. Combine the chopped eggs and the remaining ingredients.
  3. Serve on bread or with crackers.
