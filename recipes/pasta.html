Pasta

Information

Prep Time: 15 mins

Cook Time: 1 hrs 40 mins

Total Time: 1 hrs 55 mins

Servings: 16

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet italian sausage, diced
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onions
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (6.5 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seed
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions

  1. Cook sausage, beef, onion, and garlic in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat until browned; drain fat.
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes. tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water. Mix in sugar, basil, Italian seasoning, fennel seed, salt, and pepper.
  3. Cover and simmer, stirring occasionally, until cooked through, about 1 1/2 hours
